Jollof Rice Recipe

A classic West African dish full of flavor and spice

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you'll need to prepare Jollof Rice:

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Tomato paste 3 tablespoons
Fresh tomatoes (blended) 4 large
Onion (chopped) 1 large
Bell peppers (blended) 2 medium
Chicken stock 2 cups
Vegetable oil 1/4 cup
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Ginger (grated) 1 teaspoon
Salt To taste
Spices (thyme, curry powder, bay leaves) To taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot and saut√© the onions until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and tomato paste. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the blended tomatoes and bell peppers. Cook for 10-15 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add the chicken stock, thyme, curry powder, and bay leaves. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  5. Rinse the rice thoroughly and add it to the pot. Stir to combine.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice cook for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork, adjust the seasoning with salt, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Jollof Rice pairs perfectly with grilled chicken, fried plantains, or a fresh green salad. Enjoy!
