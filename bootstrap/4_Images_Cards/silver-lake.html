Rental Options

Silver Lake offers a variety of boat rental options to suit your needs:

  • Paddle Boats: Perfect for families or couples, paddle boats are easy to operate and provide a relaxing way to enjoy the lake.
  • Kayaks: For those seeking a more active experience, single and tandem kayaks are available for rent.
  • Canoes: Ideal for groups or families, canoes offer a classic boating experience.
  • Stand-Up Paddleboards (SUPs): A fun and unique way to explore the lake while getting a great workout.

Rental Hours

Boat rentals are available from late spring through early fall, depending on weather conditions:

  • Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Pricing

Rental prices vary depending on the type of boat and duration. Discounts may be available for groups or extended rentals. Please contact the rental office for the most up-to-date pricing.

Safety Guidelines

  • Life jackets are provided with all rentals and must be worn at all times.
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Follow all posted rules and regulations to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
